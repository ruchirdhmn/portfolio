About Me

My introduction

I'm a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from Jaypee University of Information Technology (JUIT), Solan, originally from Kangra, Himachal Pradesh. I completed my schooling in Hamirpur and have since developed a foundation in web development, complemented by hands-on experience through self-driven projects and internships. Last year, I completed a internship at NIT Hamirpur, where I deepened my practical knowledge. Some of my notable projects include a Restaurant Photo Tagging and a Diabetic Retinopathy Detection, Image Search Engine, Real-Estate Website, Encryption and Decryption of Text. Additionally, I authored a chapter titled "Chatbots in Healthcare" in the book "3D Modeling in Healthcare: Transforming Medicine with Advanced Technologies", which was accepted for publication after review. I'm passionate about building responsive, interactive, and visually compelling web experiences using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
